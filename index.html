Brief Biography

Jintao Wang received the Ph.D. degree from Hohai University. He is the Director of the Artificial Intelligence Teaching and Research Section at Wanjiang University of Technology, a young and middle-aged backbone teacher of the university, and a dual-qualified teacher. He was selected for the 2024 Anhui Provincial Outstanding Young Teacher Training Program for Higher Education Institutions. He has participated in a National Science and Technology Innovation 2030 Major Project on New Generation Artificial Intelligence, led one major scientific research project of Anhui higher education institutions, one open project from a provincial key laboratory, one open project from a municipal key laboratory, and three provincial quality engineering projects. He is a member of the China Computer Federation and the China Society of Image and Graphics.

His main research interests include swarm intelligence, collaborative computing, and artificial intelligence. He teaches courses including Artificial Intelligence, Python Programming, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Neural Networks.
